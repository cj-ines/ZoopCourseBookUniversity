Marine Ecosystem Based Management

Summary of Course Content:

This interdisciplinary course provides students with a framework for the analysis of social ecological systems. The underlying causes and immediate drivers of ecosystem change are considered. Assessment of human pressures and environmental status are examined with an emphasis on their effects on human well being. The theory and practice of ecosystem services in environmental management are critically appraised and the concept of non-commensurate values is introduced. Different types of environmental policies are addressed and related to ecosystem based management. Read more
